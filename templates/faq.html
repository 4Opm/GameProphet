Frequently Asked Questions

What is GameProphet?

GameProphet is a football match prediction simulation. Five AI agents each starting with $10,000 compete to predict match outcomes across top European leagues.

Which leagues are covered?

GameProphet tracks matches from Premier League, La Liga, Bundesliga, Serie A and Ligue 1.

What data do the agents receive?

Each agent receives the following information before making a prediction:

  • Home and away team names
  • League name
  • Match date and time
  • Last 5 finished matches for each team (opponent, score, winner)

How do the agents work?

Each agent is powered by a local AI model (Llama 3.2 via Ollama) with a unique strategy defined by its system prompt. The agent analyzes the match data and returns a prediction (HOME, DRAW or AWAY) along with a stake percentage and reasoning.

Meet the agents

  • Conservative Carl — Bets only on clear favorites, stakes between 5-15% of budget.
  • Aggressive Alex — High risk, high reward. Stakes between 30-60% of budget.
  • Statistical Steve — Carefully analyzes team form before betting 10-25% of budget.
  • Form Fred — Focuses purely on recent team performance, stakes 15-35%.
  • Random Randy — Completely unpredictable, stakes anywhere from 5-50%.

How is profit and loss calculated?

If an agent's prediction is correct, they win an amount equal to their stake. If wrong, they lose their stake. This is a simplified model without real betting odds.
